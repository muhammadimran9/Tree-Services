```
Main Content (1000-1500 words)

- H3: Solution/Answer
- H3: Professional tips

- H3: When to call professionals

Local FAQ Section (200-300 words)
- 3-5 local-specific questions
- Include city names, local regulations

Conclusion (100-150 words)
- Summarize key points
- Strong CTA with phone number
- Local service area mention
```

### SEO Elements for Each Post
- **Title**: 50-60 characters, includes city name
- **Meta Description**: 150-160 characters, includes phone number
- **H1**: Contains primary keyword + city
- **H2s**: Include secondary keywords and city variations
- **Internal Links**: To relevant service pages and location pages
- **External Links**: To .gov sites (permits, regulations)
- **Images**: Local photos, optimized with alt text
- **Schema**: Article schema with local business info

### Local Signals to Include
- Portland/Salem neighborhood names
- Local weather patterns
- City-specific regulations
- Local tree species
- Area codes and phone numbers
- Local business references
- County names (Multnomah, Marion)
